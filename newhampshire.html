Understanding Energy Deregulation in New Hampshire

New Hampshire was one of the first states to deregulate its electricity market, doing so in 1996. This gives consumers the freedom to choose their electric supplier. Sunara Energy is a licensed electric supplier in New Hampshire, offering competitive rates while your local utility handles delivery.

When you sign up with Sunara Energy, you'll see two charges on your bill: a supply charge from Sunara (based on your usage and rate) and a delivery charge from your local utility (for maintaining lines and delivering power). Switching is easy and your power is never interrupted.
