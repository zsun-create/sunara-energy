Understanding Energy Deregulation in Texas

Since the late 1990s, Texas residents have had the freedom to choose their own energy providers. The deregulated Texas market means you're not locked into one utility — you choose the provider and rate that works best for you. Sunara Energy is a licensed Retail Electric Provider (REP) that purchases energy and sells it directly to you at competitive fixed rates.

When you sign up with Sunara Energy, you'll see two charges on your bill: a supply charge from Sunara (based on your usage and rate) and a delivery charge from your local utility (for maintaining lines and delivering power). Switching is easy and your power is never interrupted.
