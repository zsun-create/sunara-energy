Understanding Energy Deregulation in Maryland

Maryland joined several other states by deregulating its energy market in the late 1990s. This means Maryland consumers can choose their electricity supplier instead of being locked into one utility provider. Sunara Power is a licensed supplier in Maryland, offering competitive rates while your utility continues delivering power.

When you sign up with Sunara Power, you'll see two charges on your bill: a supply charge from Sunara (based on your usage and rate) and a delivery charge from your local utility (for maintaining lines and delivering power). Switching is easy and your power is never interrupted.
