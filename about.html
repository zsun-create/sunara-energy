Why Sunara Power?

Built for homes and businesses across deregulated energy markets. Powered by transparency. Driven by service.

Electricity That Works For You

Sunara Power was founded on a simple idea: homes and businesses deserve affordable, reliable electricity without the confusion, hidden fees, or gimmicks that come with most utility companies.

We are a retail electricity provider (REP) serving both residential customers and businesses of all sizes — from single-family homes to multi-location commercial operations across deregulated energy markets.

Whether you are a homeowner looking to lower your monthly bill, a small business watching overhead costs, or an enterprise needing a dedicated energy partner — Sunara Power has a plan built for you.
